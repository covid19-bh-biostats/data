The United Nations Statistics Division collects from all the National
Statistical Offices several population censuses' datasets. The data are
collected via the Demographic Yearbook census questionnaires. The censuses'
datasets reported by the National Statistical Offices for the censuses
conducted worldwide during the period 1995.

source: https://unstats.un.org/unsd/demographic-social/products/dyb/index.cshtml/#censusdatasets
